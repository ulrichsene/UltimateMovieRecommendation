About Free2MeMovies

Our Website

Free 2 Me Movies is a movie recommendation system that recommends movies that are free to you!

Your Streaming Services

When you select the streaming services you have access to, we will only recommend you movies that are currently available on those streaming services. This way, our movie recommendations will be free to you!

Getting Recommendations

Enter the name of a movie you like, and click "Get Recommendations" to see movies that are free on your selected services.
